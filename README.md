National Parks of California                                                                      
============================

Description
============================
The website dedicated to US National Parks in California enables users to explore the offerings of each park. Users can click on individual cards that provide general information, display reviews from previous visitors, and allow new users to contribute their own reviews. Additionally, the site features a Trip Planner tool, enabling users to plan their visits based on activity preferences and the chosen season. A dedicated Contact page is available for users seeking assistance, and it also provides links to the site's social media pages for further engagement.

Future Dev Ideas
============================

* Multimedia Content: Enables users to generate and share their own photos and videos for each park upon submission.

* Account Features: Incorporate a user login and authentication system to enable the creation of personalized accounts, allowing users to access their saved reviews and preferences.

* Newsletter Subscription: Allow users to subscribe to newsletters for updates on park events, activities, and news.

* Review Comments: Enable users to comment on and upvote or like other users' reviews, creating more community engagement.

Features:
============================
**Home:**

* Brief introduction to the National Parks located in California.
* Overview of website's intended purpose.
* Image gallery featuring the stunning scenary that can be found in the Parks.


**Parks Page**

* Users can view individual cards for each park.
* Clicking on the "View Park" option renders park-specific information for the selected park.
* In the "View Park" section, users access a concise description, activities, and wildlife details unique to the chosen park.
* General information about the park is also presented.
* Additionally, there is a submission form allowing users to contribute to existing reviews submitted by other members.

  
**Trip Planner:**

* The website includes a Trip Planner tool.
* Users have the option to select a season and an activity of interest.
* The website then suggests a park with recommended activities and lodging that aligns with the user's chosen criteria.
* This feature aids visitors in planning their trips based on their interests and the chosen time of year.


**Contact Page:**

* Users can make inquiries or seek assistance by calling the California National Park.
* An email option is available for written communication.
* Visitors can access links to the official social media pages, promoting engagement and updates.
